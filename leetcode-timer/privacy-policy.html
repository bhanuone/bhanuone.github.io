LeetCode Timer - Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following information when you use our extension:

  • The LeetCode problem you are working on
  • The time you start working on the problem
  • The time you submit your solution
  • Your progress on the problem (e.g., if you have solved the problem or not)

This information is collected automatically when you use the extension. You do not need to provide any additional information.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to provide the features of the extension, such as tracking your coding time and setting time limits. We may also use your information to improve the extension and to generate statistics on how users are using the extension.

How do we protect your information?

We take your privacy very seriously and we have implemented a number of measures to protect your information. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information when it is transmitted and when it is stored. We also have a number of security policies and procedures in place to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Will we share your information with anyone else?

We will never share your information with any third parties. We use your information only for the purposes described above.

How long do we keep your information?

We keep your information for as long as you are using the extension. Once you uninstall the extension, we will delete your information within 30 days.

Your choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • You can choose not to use the extension.
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time.
  • You can request to see your information.
  • You can request to have your information deleted.

Changes to this privacy policy
